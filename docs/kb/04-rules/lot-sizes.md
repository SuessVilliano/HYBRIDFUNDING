## What is 1 lot equal to?

| Instrument | 1 lot = |
|---|---|
| Forex | $100,000 notional |
| Index | 1 contract |
| **SPX500** (exception) | 10 contracts |
| **JPN225** (exception) | 500 contracts |
| Cryptos | 1 coin |
| Silver | 5,000 ounces |
| Gold | 100 ounces |
| Oil | 100 barrels |

These standards apply to Forex/CFD trading on DXtrade, MatchTrader, and cTrader.

## Why it matters

Position size in lots maps directly to notional exposure and required margin. Pay attention to the per-instrument multiplier — opening "1 lot of SPX500" is **10 contracts**, not 1.

## Common questions

**Q:** Does this apply to Single Session Equities?
**A:** No. SSE is share-based, not lot-based. Commissions are **$0.02 per share per side** with a **$0.50 minimum per transaction**. See [Single Session Equities](../02-programs/single-session-equities.md).
