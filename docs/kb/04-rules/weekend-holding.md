## What it is

By default, all positions must be **closed by 3:45 PM EST on the last trading day of the week** (typically Friday). Any positions left open after this time will be **automatically closed** by the system.

Holding a position past 3:45 PM EST without the Weekend Hold add-on is a **soft breach** — the violating trades are closed, but the account itself is not terminated and you can keep trading when markets reopen.

**Only Crypto instruments are available for weekend trading.**

## When the cutoff shifts

In weeks where markets close early or are not open on a Friday (e.g. holidays), it's the **trader's responsibility** to ensure all trades are closed before the weekend, based on the adjusted market schedule. The system will still attempt to auto-close at 3:45 PM EST.

## The Weekend Hold add-on

Available **at point of sale only** as an optional add-on:

- **Instant Funding:** Hold Over Weekend add-on costs **+10% of the plan price**. Disables the Flat for Weekend requirement so positions can stay open over the weekend.

Without this add-on purchased at checkout, the default Friday cutoff applies.

## Common questions

**Q:** What happens if my position is closed by the auto-flat at 3:45 PM EST?
**A:** The trade is closed at market. Any realized P&L from that close is reflected in your account. This counts as a soft breach — you can resume trading when markets reopen.

**Q:** Can I trade over the weekend?
**A:** Only Crypto instruments are available for weekend trading, and only if you have the Weekend Hold add-on (or the Hold Over Weekend add-on on Instant Funding) active on your account.

**Q:** Does this apply to Single Session Equities?
**A:** No. SSE has its own 15:55 ET intraday cutoff — see [Single Session Equities](../02-programs/single-session-equities.md).
