## What it is

Copy trading from one account to another is **only permitted if the trader owns all of the accounts involved**.

## What's not allowed

- **Group trading** — multiple traders sharing a single strategy or coordinated execution across separately-owned accounts
- **Signal services** — receiving signals from a third party and executing them on Hybrid Funding accounts
- **Passing services** — paying someone to trade your evaluation for you
- Any method that bypasses **individual strategy** ownership and execution

## What is allowed

- Mirroring trades **from one of your own accounts to another of your own accounts**. (Subject to all other rules — Prohibited Trading, news events, minimum hold time, etc.)
- Using **Hybrid CopyTrade** within your own ecosystem of personally-owned accounts.

## Common questions

**Q:** I want to subscribe to a signal provider. Can I copy their trades into my Hybrid Funding account?
**A:** No. Receiving trades from a third party violates the rule that the strategy must be the trader's own.

**Q:** Can my friend and I both use the same EA?
**A:** You can each separately license and use an EA, but the rule against **off-the-shelf or third-party strategies** still applies, and Hybrid Funding may review trading patterns that look coordinated across multiple accounts.
