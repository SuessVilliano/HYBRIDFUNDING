## What it is

Hybrid Funding is part of a broader trading ecosystem. Every funded Hybrid Funding trader gets access to tools and a community that most prop firms don't offer.

## The ecosystem

- **Hybrid Funding** — proprietary trading firm and funded accounts. [hybridfunding.co](https://hybridfunding.co)
- **Hybrid Journal** — free trading journal and analytics tool. [hybridjournal.co](https://hybridjournal.co)
- **Hybrid CopyTrade** — signal syncing and trade copying. [copy.tradehybrid.co](https://copy.tradehybrid.co)
- **The Hybrid Zone** — paid trading community and education hub. [tradehybrid.co](https://tradehybrid.co)

## Common questions

**Q:** Do I have to use the other tools to get funded?
**A:** No. Hybrid Journal, Hybrid CopyTrade, and The Hybrid Zone are independent products. They are offered as benefits to funded traders, but they are not required to participate in the Hybrid Funding program.

**Q:** Is The Hybrid Zone included with my funded account?
**A:** No, The Hybrid Zone is a separate paid community. Funded traders sometimes receive promotional access or discounts; check current offers on [tradehybrid.co](https://tradehybrid.co).
