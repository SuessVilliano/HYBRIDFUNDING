## What Hybrid Funding is

Hybrid Funding is a proprietary trading firm that funds traders with firm capital after they demonstrate consistent, disciplined trading in an evaluation phase (or, for Instant Funding plans, immediately on purchase). We operate as Hybrid Funding LLC and are headquartered in the United States.

We're built for serious traders who want a clear, rules-driven path to funded payouts — not just another challenge that's designed to be failed.

## What we offer

- **One-Step Evaluation** — single-phase evaluation, then a funded account.
- **Two-Step Evaluation** — two-phase evaluation with higher leverage caps available.
- **Instant Funding** — skip evaluation entirely and start with a funded account.
- **Single Session Equities** — intraday-only S&P 100 equity trading with its own ruleset.

Each program is detailed in its own article under `02-programs/`.

## Markets and platforms

Hybrid Funding supports trading on **DXtrade**, **MatchTrader**, and **cTrader** for Forex, CFD Indices, Metals, and Cryptocurrencies. Our **Single Session Equities** program runs on the **GooeyPro** trading platform exclusively.

## How we make money

We provide capital and take a share of trading profits. Profit splits vary by program — see [Profit Splits](../06-funding-and-payouts/profit-splits.md) for the authoritative breakdown per program.

## Common questions

**Q:** Is Hybrid Funding a broker?
**A:** No. Hybrid Funding is a proprietary trading firm. Brokerage, liquidity, and execution are handled by our liquidity provider; we provide the capital, the rules, and the payout framework.

**Q:** Where can I learn more or sign up?
**A:** Visit [https://www.hybridfunding.co](https://www.hybridfunding.co) for current programs and pricing.
