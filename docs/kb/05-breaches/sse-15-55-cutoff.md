## What it is

In Single Session Equities, **all positions must be fully closed by 15:55 ET**. A position open past 15:55 ET is treated as a violation of the Prohibited Practices rule and results in a **hard breach** — the account is terminated.

## How the platform handles it

- The platform attempts to automatically close all open positions at 15:55 ET.
- The responsibility to close on time is **always the trader's**, not the platform's.
- If a position remains open past 15:55 ET (for any reason), the account is hard-breached.

## How to comply

- Set alerts before the close.
- Never enter a position late in the session that you don't plan to exit cleanly before 15:55 ET.
- Account for sudden volatility near the close — wide spreads or fast tape can make late-session exits harder.

## Common questions

**Q:** What if the platform's auto-close fails?
**A:** The responsibility is the trader's. If you can demonstrate platform malfunction with timestamps and error messages, contact support@hybridfunding.io — but the default outcome is a hard breach.

**Q:** Can I trade during Pre-Market or Extended Hours?
**A:** No. Only the 09:30 ET – 15:55 ET window is permitted in SSE.

**Q:** Does this rule apply during the Evaluation phase?
**A:** Yes. The 15:55 ET cutoff applies in both Evaluation and Funded phases.
