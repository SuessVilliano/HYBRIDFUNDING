## Where to track progress

When you purchase a plan, you receive access to a **trader dashboard**. The dashboard is updated in near real time as your account metrics are calculated.

## What the dashboard shows

- Current balance and equity
- Progress toward profit target (Evaluation phase)
- Current Daily Loss Limit threshold
- Current Max Drawdown threshold
- Minimum profitable trading days (SSE)
- Days remaining before inactivity rule triggers
- Withdrawal history and next eligible date

## Your responsibility

**It is your responsibility to monitor your breach levels.** The dashboard surfaces the numbers, but the trader is responsible for staying within the rules. Auto-flat at 3:45 PM EST (Forex/CFD) and 15:55 ET (SSE) are safety nets, not rule-replacements.

## Common questions

**Q:** Is there a mobile app?
**A:** The trader dashboard is web-based and accessible from mobile browsers. Trading happens in the platform (DXtrade, MatchTrader, cTrader, or GooeyPro), each of which has its own mobile app.

**Q:** What if the dashboard shows the wrong balance?
**A:** Dashboard data is updated in near real time but may briefly lag the platform during high-volume periods. If the discrepancy persists, contact support@hybridfunding.io with timestamps and screenshots.
