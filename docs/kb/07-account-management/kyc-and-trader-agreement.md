## What it is

**KYC** (Know Your Customer) is the identity verification process required before any payout. **The Trader Agreement** is the contract governing your funded account. Both are mandatory.

## When you have to complete them

| Program | KYC + Trader Agreement timing |
|---|---|
| One-Step Evaluation | After passing the evaluation, before the funded account is issued |
| Two-Step Evaluation | After passing both phases, before the funded account is issued |
| Instant Funding | Not required upfront; **required before your first withdrawal** |

## What happens if KYC fails

- **One-Step / Two-Step:** the funded account is not issued. If you can't pass KYC, you can't get a funded account regardless of evaluation performance.
- **Instant Funding:** the withdrawal request is rejected **and the account is closed**.

Make sure you can meet KYC requirements **before** purchasing — especially for Instant Funding, where the cost is sunk if you can't pass KYC at withdrawal time.

## What's typically needed

- A valid government-issued photo ID
- A recent proof of address (utility bill, bank statement, etc.)
- Selfie or live-check (depending on the KYC provider's flow)

The exact requirements are presented inside the KYC flow when you start it.

## Common questions

**Q:** I'm under 18 — can I still buy a plan?
**A:** No. You must be at least 18 years of age, or the applicable minimum legal age in your country, to purchase a plan.

**Q:** I live in an OFAC-listed country. Can I sign up?
**A:** No. Traders from OFAC-listed countries are not accepted. See [Countries and Age Eligibility](../09-eligibility-and-affiliates/countries-and-age.md).

**Q:** I'm partway through KYC and something keeps failing — what do I do?
**A:** Contact support@hybridfunding.io with your account ID and a description of where the KYC flow is breaking.
