## How taxes work

When trading a funded account for Hybrid Funding, you are treated as an **independent contractor**. As a result, **you are responsible for any and all taxes** on your gains.

## What this means in practice

- We do not withhold taxes from your payouts.
- You should expect to handle your own tax reporting based on the rules of your tax jurisdiction.
- Talk to a tax professional for personalized advice — Hybrid Funding cannot provide tax guidance.

## U.S. traders

Funded payouts may be reportable income for U.S. tax purposes. Consult a CPA familiar with self-employed / independent contractor income.

## International traders

Tax treatment varies significantly by country. Consult a local tax professional for guidance specific to your jurisdiction.

## Common questions

**Q:** Will I get a 1099?
**A:** Depending on your country, payout total, and applicable thresholds, you may receive a tax form. Specific 1099 issuance is not guaranteed — track your own income and consult a tax professional.

**Q:** Can I expense my plan purchase?
**A:** Whether plan-purchase costs are deductible depends on your tax situation. Ask your tax advisor.
