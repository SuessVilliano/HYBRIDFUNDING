## How charges appear

Charges from Hybrid Funding appear on bank and credit card statements as:

> **Dashboardanalytix.com**

This is the merchant of record for plan purchases and add-on charges. If you see a charge from Dashboardanalytix.com that you don't recognize, it's almost certainly a Hybrid Funding plan or add-on purchase.

## Refunds

Refund eligibility depends on the plan, when it was purchased, and whether the account has been used. Contact support@hybridfunding.io with your account ID and order details to ask about a specific refund situation.

## Disputed charges

If you believe a charge is unauthorized, contact us **before** filing a chargeback. Filing a chargeback without prior contact may result in account termination. We will work with you to resolve any charge dispute directly.

## Common questions

**Q:** Is Dashboardanalytix.com a different company?
**A:** Dashboardanalytix.com is the merchant of record for Hybrid Funding plan purchases. It's how the charge surfaces on your statement.

**Q:** Where do I see my payment history?
**A:** Your trader dashboard shows your account purchases and any add-ons applied. For card statement detail, check directly with your card issuer.
