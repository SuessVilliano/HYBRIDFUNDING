## What it is

The Instant Funding Plan lets traders start with a fully funded account immediately on purchase — no evaluation phase required. You skip the assessment and trade firm capital from day one, subject to the program's risk rules.

## Rule summary

| Rule | Value |
|---|---|
| Profit Target | None (no profit limit) |
| Daily Loss Limit | 5% (equity-based, prior-day balance, **Hard Breach**) |
| Max Drawdown | 8% trailing, locks at starting balance at payout (**Hard Breach**) |
| Leverage (Forex / Metals) | Up to 50:1 |
| Leverage (Indices) | Up to 10:1 |
| Leverage (Oil) | Up to 5:1 |
| Leverage (Crypto) | Up to 2:1 |
| Inactivity | 30 days = Hard Breach |
| Weekend holding | Flat by 3:45 PM EST Friday (Weekend Hold add-on available) |
| Max trade time | None |
| Profit Split | 80% trader / 20% firm (90% with add-on) |

## How drawdown works

The Max Drawdown is an **8% trailing** drawdown based on **closed balance** (not equity). It trails until you've achieved an 8% return, at which point it locks permanently at the starting account balance. At payout, the drawdown also locks at the starting balance.

**Example:** Starting balance $100,000. Initial drawdown floor: $92,000. If closed balance reaches $102,000, the new floor is $94,000. If closed balance reaches $106,000, the floor permanently locks at the starting $100,000. From that point on, regardless of how high the account goes, the account is breached only if equity falls to $100,000 (subject to the 5% daily loss limit also applying).

See [Max Drawdown (Trailing)](../04-rules/max-drawdown-trailing.md) for full mechanics.

## KYC timing

You can start trading immediately without completing KYC or signing a Trader Agreement. Both are required **before your first withdrawal** is processed. If you fail KYC at withdrawal time, the withdrawal is rejected and the account is closed — so make sure you can meet KYC requirements before purchasing.

## Add-ons available at purchase

- **Hold Over Weekend** (+10% of plan cost) — disables the "Flat for Weekend" requirement; positions can stay open over the weekend.
- **Profit Share Increased to 90%** (+20% of plan cost) — bumps your standard 80% profit share to 90%.

See [Add-ons](../08-add-ons/).

## Account limits

A maximum of **$190,000 in active Instant Funding plans per person** is permitted, consisting of up to one of each plan size. See [Account Limits](../07-account-management/account-limits.md).

## Common questions

**Q:** How long does it take to receive my Instant Funded Account?
**A:** Immediately on payment. You'll receive an email with instructions on how to access the account on the platform you chose at checkout (DXtrade, MatchTrader, or cTrader).

**Q:** Is this a demo or live account?
**A:** Your Instant Funded Account is backed by our capital and is notionally funded. See [Notional Funding](../06-funding-and-payouts/notional-funding.md).

**Q:** When can I request my first withdrawal?
**A:** You can request a withdrawal once per 30 days. Minimum withdrawal is the greater of $100 or 1% of the account's starting balance. See [Withdrawal Process](../06-funding-and-payouts/withdrawal-process.md).
