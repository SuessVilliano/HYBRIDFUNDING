## What it is

The Two-Step Evaluation is a two-phase challenge. You complete two evaluation phases, each with its own profit target and drawdown rules, before you receive a funded account. The Two-Step is structured for traders who want a more measured demonstration of consistency and who want access to higher leverage in Forex and Metals.

## Leverage caps

Two-Step plans can offer leverage up to **30:1 on Forex and Metals** (versus the One-Step cap of 20:1). Other asset class leverage caps are:

- **10:1 leverage** on Indices
- **5:1 leverage** on Oil
- **2:1 leverage** on Cryptocurrencies

## Rules that apply

The Two-Step Evaluation follows the same core rules that apply to the Funded Account:

- Daily Loss Limit — see [Daily Loss Limit](../04-rules/daily-loss-limit.md)
- Max Trailing Drawdown — see [Max Drawdown (Trailing)](../04-rules/max-drawdown-trailing.md)
- 30-day inactivity rule — see [Inactivity Rule](../04-rules/inactivity-rule.md)
- Weekend close-out — see [Weekend Holding](../04-rules/weekend-holding.md)
- News event prohibition — see [News Events](../04-rules/news-events.md)
- No prohibited trading strategies — see [Prohibited Trading](../04-rules/prohibited-trading.md)

The specific profit target percentage for each phase and the trailing drawdown percentage depend on the account size and plan you purchase. Both are visible on your trader dashboard for any active plan.

## After you pass

Once you complete both evaluation phases, you'll receive an email with instructions to complete your KYC verification and Trader Agreement. Once both are complete, your funded account is typically created and issued within 24–48 business hours. See [Funded Account Delivery](../06-funding-and-payouts/funded-account-delivery.md).

## Common questions

**Q:** Why pick Two-Step over One-Step?
**A:** Two-Step offers higher leverage caps on Forex and Metals and spreads the proof-of-consistency requirement across two phases, which some traders prefer over a single-phase pass/fail. The right choice depends on your trading style, target market, and how much leverage you need.

**Q:** Is there a time limit to pass each phase?
**A:** Beyond the 30-day inactivity rule, there is no maximum time limit to complete either phase.

**Q:** Can I have multiple Two-Step plans at once?
**A:** See [Account Limits](../07-account-management/account-limits.md). You can have one Two-Step plan of a given account size active across all platforms.
