## What it is

A point-of-sale add-on for **Single Session Equities** plans that **protects a trader's eligible profit share in the event of a hard breach** of the funded account.

## Cost

**+25% of the plan price**, added at checkout.

## When to consider it

- You expect to push the account hard and want a safety net on your locked-in profits.
- You'd rather pay upfront for breach insurance than take the full risk of forfeiting eligible profit share on a single bad day.

## Limitations

- Available only **at point of sale**. You cannot add it after purchase.
- Specific to **Single Session Equities** plans.

## Common questions

**Q:** Does this protect against losses past the starting balance?
**A:** Payout Protector protects **eligible profit share** in the event of a hard breach. It does not change the trailing drawdown floor — losses that bring the account below the trailing drawdown threshold still trigger a breach.

**Q:** Is this the same as Lock Upon Payout?
**A:** No. Lock Upon Payout is a separate setting (default ON in SSE; +25% add-on to disable). Payout Protector specifically covers profit-share protection on a hard breach. They're independent.
