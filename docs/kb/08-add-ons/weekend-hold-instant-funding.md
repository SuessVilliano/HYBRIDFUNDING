## What it is

A point-of-sale add-on for **Instant Funding** plans that **disables the Flat for Weekend requirement**. With this add-on, positions can stay open over the weekend.

## Cost

**+10% of the plan price**, added at checkout.

## When to consider it

- You trade strategies that intentionally hold positions over the weekend.
- You're trading instruments (typically Crypto) that have weekend liquidity.
- You're regularly working into Friday's close and don't want to be forced into auto-flat positions.

## Limitations

- Available only **at point of sale**. You cannot add it after purchase.
- Without this add-on, the default 3:45 PM EST Friday auto-flat applies. See [Weekend Holding](../04-rules/weekend-holding.md).

## Common questions

**Q:** Can I add this on later?
**A:** No — it's a point-of-sale add-on only.

**Q:** Does this exist for One-Step / Two-Step Evaluation plans?
**A:** The Hold Over Weekend +10% add-on is specifically documented for Instant Funding. For weekend holding on evaluation plans, the general Weekend Hold add-on concept may apply — confirm at checkout for the specific plan you're buying.
