## What it is

A point-of-sale add-on for **Single Session Equities** plans that raises the **25% consistency requirement** for payout eligibility. With the add-on, a higher percentage of total profits can come from a single trading day without disqualifying the payout.

## Default rule (without add-on)

In the SSE Funded phase, **no more than 25% of total profits may be generated on a single trading day** to qualify for a payout.

## Cost

The exact add-on cost is shown at checkout and may vary by plan size and the consistency cap selected.

## When to consider it

- You're an aggressive intraday trader and expect occasional large single-day gains that would otherwise trip the 25% threshold and delay your payout.
- You'd rather pay a one-time fee at checkout than spread out gains across more days before requesting a payout.

## Limitations

- Available only **at point of sale**. You cannot add it after purchase.
- Specific to **Single Session Equities** plans.

## Common questions

**Q:** Does this remove the consistency rule entirely?
**A:** It raises the cap; it doesn't remove the rule entirely. The specific new cap is shown at checkout.

**Q:** Does it apply during the Evaluation phase too?
**A:** The consistency rule is a Funded-phase payout-eligibility check, so the add-on impacts payout eligibility once you're in the Funded phase.
