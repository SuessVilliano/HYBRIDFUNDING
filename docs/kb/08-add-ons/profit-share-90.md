## What it is

A point-of-sale add-on for **Instant Funding** plans that bumps the trader's profit share from the standard **80%** to **90%** of profits.

## Cost

**+20% of the plan price**, added at checkout.

## Worked example

| | Without add-on (80/20) | With add-on (90/10) |
|---|---|---|
| $10,000 in withdrawable gains | Trader receives **$8,000** | Trader receives **$9,000** |
| $50,000 in withdrawable gains | Trader receives **$40,000** | Trader receives **$45,000** |

## When to consider it

- You expect to take material withdrawals from the funded account.
- The +20% upfront cost pays for itself once your cumulative gains hit roughly the same dollar value as the price difference (rough break-even depends on plan size).

## Limitations

- Available only **at point of sale**. You cannot add it after purchase.
- Only available on **Instant Funding** plans currently.

## Common questions

**Q:** Does this add-on stack with any other profit-share boosts?
**A:** The 90% share is the current cap. There is no further bump beyond 90% under our published programs.

**Q:** Can I get this on the Single Session Equities program?
**A:** The Profit Share 90% add-on is specifically documented for Instant Funding. SSE's standard split is 80/20 and does not currently have an equivalent published add-on.
