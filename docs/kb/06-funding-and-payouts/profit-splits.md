## Profit splits by program

| Program | Profit split (standard) | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Instant Funding | **80% trader / 20% firm** | Can increase to 90/10 with the [Profit Share Increased to 90% add-on](../08-add-ons/profit-share-90.md) at +20% of plan price |
| Single Session Equities | **80% trader / 20% firm** | — |
| One-Step Evaluation | See worked example below — the rules doc's withdrawal example uses a **75/25** ratio | Confirm the exact split for your plan on the trader dashboard or with support |
| Two-Step Evaluation | Same caveat as One-Step | Confirm on dashboard or with support |

### Why the One-Step / Two-Step row is hedged

The Instant Funding rules doc and the Single Session Equities rules doc **explicitly** state 80/20. The published One-Step / Two-Step rules doc does **not** state a numeric profit split as a top-line rule; the only signal is a worked withdrawal example, which uses **75/25** ($12,000 to trader, $4,000 to firm, on a $16,000 withdrawal). Because the rule isn't stated explicitly in that doc, this KB defers to the trader's dashboard and to support for the authoritative split on any specific One-Step or Two-Step plan.

## When the split is applied

The split is applied at the time of withdrawal. The trader's share is paid out; the firm's share is simultaneously withdrawn from the funded account. Both leave the account at the same time.

## Worked example — Instant Funding (80/20 standard)

- Account grows from $100,000 to $120,000 → gain = $20,000
- Trader requests a **$16,000** withdrawal
- Trader receives **$12,800** (80% of $16,000)
- Hybrid Funding retains **$3,200** (20% of $16,000)
- Account balance after withdrawal: **$104,000**
- Max Trailing Drawdown locks at the starting $100,000 — see [Withdrawal and Trailing Drawdown](withdrawal-and-trailing-drawdown.md)

## Worked example — One-Step / Two-Step Evaluation (per WL1 rules doc example)

- Account grows from $100,000 to $120,000 → gain = $20,000
- Trader requests a **$16,000** withdrawal
- Trader receives **$12,000**
- Hybrid Funding retains **$4,000**
- Account balance after withdrawal: **$104,000**
- Max Trailing Drawdown locks at the starting $100,000

## Common questions

**Q:** Why are the One-Step / Two-Step example and the Instant Funding example different?
**A:** They come from different source rules docs. The Instant Funding rules explicitly state an 80/20 split. The One-Step / Two-Step rules don't state a numeric split as a top-line rule but include a worked example using 75/25. If you're on a One-Step or Two-Step plan, **check your trader dashboard** for the authoritative split on your plan, or email support@hybridfunding.io to confirm.

**Q:** Can I negotiate a different split?
**A:** The published splits are the splits. The only published path to a higher trader share is the Profit Share Increased to 90% add-on for **Instant Funding**, purchased at checkout.
