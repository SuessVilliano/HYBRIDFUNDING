## How fast you get your funded account

### One-Step and Two-Step Evaluations

After passing your evaluation, you'll receive an email with instructions to complete:

1. **KYC verification** — Know Your Customer identity check
2. **Trader Agreement** — sign the agreement governing your funded account

Once both are completed and supporting documentation is provided, your funded account is **typically created, funded, and issued within 24–48 business hours**. You'll receive a confirmation email when the account is enabled.

### Instant Funding

Your Instant Funded Account is delivered **immediately on payment**. You'll receive an email with login instructions for the platform you chose at checkout (DXtrade, MatchTrader, or cTrader).

**KYC and Trader Agreement are NOT required upfront** for Instant Funding — but both must be completed **before your first withdrawal** is processed. If you fail KYC at withdrawal time, the withdrawal is rejected and the account is closed.

## Is the funded account a demo or live account?

The capital in your funded account is **notional**. See [Notional Funding](notional-funding.md) for what that means. In practical terms: market pricing and trade execution are real, and your P&L is real — the capital is provided by Hybrid Funding rather than being your own deposited cash.

## Common questions

**Q:** Why does it take 24–48 hours after passing the evaluation?
**A:** That window covers KYC review, Trader Agreement processing, and the technical creation of the funded account. As long as your KYC and agreement are submitted promptly, you should be live within that timeframe.

**Q:** What if I fail KYC?
**A:** If KYC fails on a One-Step or Two-Step funded-account issuance, the funded account is not provisioned. For Instant Funding, failing KYC at withdrawal time causes both the withdrawal request to be rejected and the account to be closed. **Make sure you can meet KYC requirements before purchasing.**

**Q:** What documents do I need for KYC?
**A:** Typically a government-issued photo ID and a recent proof of address. The exact requirements are presented inside the KYC flow when you start it.
