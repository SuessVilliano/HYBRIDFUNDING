## Cadence

- **First payout:** requestable after **14 days** in the Funded phase
- **Subsequent payouts:** every **14 days**, provided all conditions are met
- **Minimum withdrawal:** **$100**
- **Profit Split:** 80% trader / 20% firm

## Conditions for payout eligibility

To request a payout in SSE, you must satisfy:

1. **Time-in-funded:** at least 14 days have passed (since funding or since last approved payout)
2. **Minimum profitable trading days:** at least **3 separate trading days** with **≥ 0.5% profit each**
3. **Consistency Rule:** no more than **25% of total profits** generated on a single trading day (unless the consistency-add-on raises this; see [Consistency Rule Add-on](../08-add-ons/consistency-rule-add-on.md))
4. **All program rules** met (no hard breaches; 15:55 ET cutoff respected; minimum hold time respected; etc.)

## Lock Upon Payout

By default, **Lock Upon Payout is ON**: once a payout is processed, the Max Drawdown is permanently locked at the original starting balance. See [Lock Upon Payout (SSE)](lock-upon-payout-sse.md).

There's an optional add-on at checkout — **+25% of plan price** — to disable Lock Upon Payout.

## Payout Protector add-on

Optional add-on at checkout — **+25% of plan price** — that protects a trader's eligible profit share in the event of a hard breach. See [Payout Protector](../08-add-ons/payout-protector.md).

## Common questions

**Q:** What if I'm under the consistency limit on every payout request — does that limit my upside?
**A:** The consistency rule is a payout-eligibility check, not a trading restriction. You can still trade however you want; just be aware that to qualify for a payout, your profits must be spread across multiple days so that no single day represents more than 25% of total profits.

**Q:** Can I request a payout earlier than 14 days if I've already hit the consistency and profitable-days requirements?
**A:** No. The first payout requires at least 14 days in the Funded phase regardless of how quickly you meet the other conditions.

**Q:** How does the payout get to me?
**A:** Through the available outbound payment methods in the trader dashboard at the time of request.
