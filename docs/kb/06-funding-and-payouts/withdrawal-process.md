## How to withdraw

Traders request withdrawals through the **trader dashboard**. Click **Withdraw Profits** in the dashboard and enter the amount you want to withdraw.

## Cadence and minimums

| Program | First payout | Subsequent payouts | Minimum withdrawal |
|---|---|---|---|
| One-Step / Two-Step (funded) | At any time after funding | Every 30 days | Standard minimum (see your plan) |
| Instant Funding | After 30 days | Every 30 days | **Greater of $100 or 1% of starting balance** |
| Single Session Equities | After **14 days** in Funded phase | Every **14 days** | **$100** |

Withdrawals are limited to one approved request per cadence window.

## What happens when a withdrawal is approved

1. Hybrid Funding processes the trader's share of the gains per the profit split.
2. Hybrid Funding withdraws its share at the same time.
3. The trader's **Max Trailing Drawdown locks at the starting balance**. See [Withdrawal and Trailing Drawdown](withdrawal-and-trailing-drawdown.md).
4. Funds are sent via the available outbound payment methods at that time.

## KYC required

KYC and Trader Agreement must be completed before any withdrawal is processed:

- **One-Step / Two-Step:** KYC is required as part of funded account issuance, so it's complete by the time you can withdraw.
- **Instant Funding:** KYC is deferred until your first withdrawal request. **If you fail KYC at withdrawal time, the withdrawal is rejected and the account is closed.** Make sure you can meet KYC requirements before purchasing.

## Payment methods

Outbound payment methods are offered by Hybrid Funding from time to time. Hybrid Funding reserves the right to change withdrawal methods and options at any time. Current methods are shown in the trader dashboard at the time of request.

## Common questions

**Q:** Can I withdraw all my gains at once?
**A:** Technically yes, but be aware that the Max Trailing Drawdown locks at your starting balance on withdrawal — a **full withdrawal of all gains** will simultaneously trigger the trailing-drawdown breach and **forfeit the funded account**. Always leave a buffer above the starting balance. See [Withdrawal and Trailing Drawdown](withdrawal-and-trailing-drawdown.md).

**Q:** How long does the withdrawal take after approval?
**A:** Processing time depends on the payment method. Specific timelines are shown when you initiate the withdrawal in the dashboard.

**Q:** I see "Dashboardanalytix.com" on my bank/credit card statement. What is that?
**A:** That's how charges from Hybrid Funding appear on statements. See [Billing & Statements](../07-account-management/billing-statement.md).
