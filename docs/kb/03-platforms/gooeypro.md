## What it is

GooeyPro is the exclusive trading platform for our Single Session Equities program. It's an intraday equities platform with liquidity, pricing, and execution sourced directly from Nasdaq.

## Notes

- GooeyPro is only used for Single Session Equities. Forex, Crypto, and Futures-style products are not available on GooeyPro within our programs.
- The permitted SSE trading window is **09:30 ET – 15:55 ET**. All positions must be closed by 15:55 ET. See [Single Session Equities](../02-programs/single-session-equities.md).
- Commissions on SSE: **$0.02 per share per side**, with a **$0.50 minimum per transaction**.
