## What it is

cTrader is one of the three trading platforms supported for Forex, CFD Indices, Metals, and Cryptocurrencies across our One-Step, Two-Step, and Instant Funding programs.

## How to find trading hours

Open the Symbol Window → scroll down to **Market Hours** for the selected symbol.

## Notes

- cTrader is not available for Single Session Equities. SSE is on GooeyPro only.
- Holidays can affect available trading hours.
