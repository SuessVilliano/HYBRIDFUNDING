## What it is

DXtrade is one of the three trading platforms supported for Forex, CFD Indices, Metals, and Cryptocurrencies across our One-Step, Two-Step, and Instant Funding programs.

## How to find trading hours

Right-click the symbol → select **Instrument Info**.

## Notes

- DXtrade is not available for Single Session Equities. SSE is on GooeyPro only.
- Holidays can affect available trading hours.
