## How affiliates get credited

Affiliates are credited for referrals when a user creates an account using a **link or discount code provided by the Affiliate**.

## How to apply

Apply to the affiliate program through the **Affiliate** page on [hybridfunding.co/affiliate](https://www.hybridfunding.co/affiliate).

## Common questions

**Q:** How are payouts to affiliates handled?
**A:** Specifics on commission structure and payout cadence are provided when you're accepted into the program. Apply via the affiliate page on the website.

**Q:** Can I be both an affiliate and a trader?
**A:** Yes. The two programs are independent. Make sure your trading activity does not violate the Prohibited Trading policy.
