## Countries

Subject to compliance with applicable laws and regulations, **traders from all countries except OFAC-listed countries** can participate in our programs, unless otherwise limited at Hybrid Funding's discretion.

If you're unsure whether your country is supported, you can purchase a plan only if you can meet KYC at withdrawal time. **If KYC fails because of geographic restrictions, the account is closed and the withdrawal is rejected** — make sure your country is eligible before purchasing.

## Age

You must be at least **18 years of age**, or the applicable minimum legal age in your country, to purchase a plan.

## Common questions

**Q:** Where can I see the OFAC list?
**A:** The U.S. Office of Foreign Assets Control maintains the official list at the U.S. Department of the Treasury website. If your country appears there, you are not eligible.

**Q:** Can a corporate entity buy a plan?
**A:** Hybrid Funding programs are designed for individual traders. KYC processes assume an individual. If you have a specific corporate scenario, contact support@hybridfunding.io.
